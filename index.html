Mechanical engineering

Mechanical engineering is a field in science that focuses on how things move and interact. Many advancements in mechanical engineering have brought us to a new era of innovation. From the very beginning of human civillization utilizing the resources round us has been a core goal. From the greek water mills to today's car mechanical engineering is present in almost every aspect of life.We should be very passionate about this field to keep advancing humanity forward.

Fields in Mechanical engineering include:

  • Electrical engineering
  • Biomechanics
  • Aerospace
  • Robotics
  • Automative engineering

Mechanical engineering involves sub-fields such as the few stated above .Mechanical engineering also includes profound subjects like math science and physics all acting as the back bone of innovation. The understanding of these subjects has helped us gain insight in what we can really do, and elevated our imagination. We are now able to look at our environment and ask more than what its made of or what are its properties; we can now ask how we can influence its properties to our advantage.

Electrical engineering

Its the spark that gets everything turning simply put electricty and how its used in Electrical engineering is crucial to get things working the right way Today electricty is at work every where.Electricty in your cellphone,your car, your brain, your televison. Electricty has allowed humans to achieve a mechanical advantage on earth and beyond it powers almost anything that moves or needs energy. Electrical engineers are at the heart of this they work hard to find the fastest way to transimit electrical energy with minimal losses,To how to make electrical energy flow within a system like a computer or robot.

Biomechanics

Biomechanics is where the rules of physics and mechanical enginnering meet the incredible design of te human. By studying subjects like anatomy and newtonian physics it acts as a bridge to understand how living things move change direction and withstand force.This field is split into kinematics which looks at patterns and speed of motion ad kenetics which uncovers the actual muscle forces and impacts that cause us to move.Understanding how these biological sysytems work allows engineers and athletic trainers to find new ways to prevent injuries design advanced prosthetics and push the boundaries of human performance to help people live better lives.

Robotics & Aerospace

Robotics is another field that focuses on giving machines the abilty to iteract with the environment. And make decisions to influence the environment around them.Robots do this through a wide range of sensors depending on it's function, Robotics coexists with Aerospace by allowing them complete complex tasks in deep space or places where humans can't navigate or survive. Aerospace involves engineering in earth orbit or deep space.This has allowed us to do things like building rocket ships,space-rover[robots],airplanes,And the list goes on.
